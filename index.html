Cyber Security

Cybersecurity involves safeguarding computer systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage. As our reliance on digital technologies grows, robust cybersecurity measures have become essential to protect personal information, maintain organizational integrity, and uphold national security.

Key Components Of Cyber Security

  • Network Security
  • Information Security
  • Application And Operating Security

Social Impact of Cyber Security

Cyber threats pose significant risks to critical infrastructure, including healthcare, transportation, and energy sectors. Effective cybersecurity fosters trust in digital platforms, encouraging broader participation in online activities such as e-governance, online education, and digital commerce.
